06 — Brand Voice
Tone & Language
✓ Do
Use precise, data-driven language — "4:52/km pace" not "a fast pace"
Be direct and action-oriented — "Start your run" not "Begin your running journey"
Speak in the athlete's language — RPE, threshold, VO2 max, cadence
Celebrate with specifics — "New 5K PB: 21:44"
Use short, confident sentences in UI copy
✗ Don't
Don't use vague motivational fluff — avoid "you've got this!"
Don't over-explain — athletes understand their sport
Don't use passive voice in CTAs or instructions
Don't mix metaphors across the experience
Don't use ALL CAPS for emphasis — use weight instead
